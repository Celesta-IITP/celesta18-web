INTRODUCTION
The ladders we climb are the Clues we find , spanning the campus is the gamble of dice This event require participation in teams . It is a theme based event which involves reaching various checkpoints with the help of clues they collect and complete the task if allotted. The rush stops after completion of circuit.
Rules:
1. After Registration, Every team will be provided with a copy of Snake and ladder and a clue.
2. The Clue will guide them to the next hotspot where they will be asked regarding a question about the equipment they want.
3. If wrongly answered, they will suffer from time penalty. They will also throw a dice which will guide their position on the game of Snake and ladders.
4. In the game of Snake and Ladders, if they encounter a snake they should perform a special task as penalty which will be FORGIVEN if they are able to produce the Appreciation Tokens.
5. The Appreciation Tokens can either be collected from a Mentioned Spot or they will be awarded one if they had encountered a ladder in the game.
6. They will have to complete the task correctly at each hotspot to collect all the equipments required for reaching the final hotspot.
7. At the final hotspot they will be given a Simple Electronic Circuit which they have to build on the spot and Submit.
8. The team that completes all the tasks in the minimum time, that person will be the winner of Static Rush 2017.
9. The task at each hotspot along with next clue will be given randomly by checking the number of the people they have already reached by checking their signatures at the backside of the page.
10. Also their last position in the game of Snakes and Ladders will be written on the backside of the page so that the person on the next hotspot can determine the position.
