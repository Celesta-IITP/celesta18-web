INTRODUCTION
The ladders we climb are the Clues we find , spanning the campus is the gamble of dice This event require participation in teams . It is a theme based event which involves reaching various checkpoints with the help of clues they collect and complete the task if allotted. The rush stops after completion of circuit.
Rules:
1.After Registration, Every team will be provided with a copy of Snakes and ladders and a clue.
2.The Clue will guide them to the next hotspot.
3. There they will throw a dice which will guide their position on the game of Snakes and ladders.
4.In the game of Snakes and Ladders, if they encounter a snake they should perform a special fun task as penalty .
5.If the team encounter a ladder , a bonus will be awarded which will help in reducing the total time of team in static rush.
6.They will have to complete the task (if they encounter a snake ) correctly at each hotspot to move further.
7.At the final hotspot they will be given a Simple Electronic Circuit which they have to build on the spot and Submit.
8.The team that completes static rush in the minimum time, will be the winner of Static Rush 2017.
9.The task at each hotspot along with the next clue will be given randomly keeping in mind that the clue of the hotspot visited earlier is not given again.
10.Also their last position in the game of Snakes and Ladders will be written on the backside of the page so that the person on the next hotspot can determine the position.
11. Total number of members in a team should not exceed 3.
