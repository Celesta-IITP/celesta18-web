INTRODUCTION
E-Club has come up with the golden opportunity to brushen up your debating skills with the help of entrepreneurial knowledge. The event will test your speaking style, resiliency, innovation quotient, focus, etc. with other debating skills like clarity, fluency and diction in speech.
Rules:
1. Participants will be required to register with a team of two members.
2. A topic will be given beforehand which will be common for all the teams for the first round.
3. The teams selected for the second round will be given one hour to prepare for the new topic. So embrace yourself for the challenge and bring out the Orator in you !!
