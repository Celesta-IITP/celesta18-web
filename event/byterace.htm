INTRODUCTION
Speed . Accuracy . Efficiency
"Always code as if the guy who ends up maintaining your code will be a violent psychopath who knows where you live." -- Rick Osborne
ByteRace is a challenge for minds that have honed the art of problem solving. This competition presents challenges for you to solve using code. So if you get the adrenaline rush when you see a difficult problem in front of you then this is definitely for you.
Rules:
1. This is an Online event, which will be held on HackerRank/Codechef platform.
2. This is an individual event and any form of violation of platform rules will lead to disqualification.
3. Competition consists of 5 algorithmic puzzles to quench your thirst for the unsolvable.
4. The judgement of the organizers will be final.
5. Participants can participate from their room or from the CC.They need to have a HackerRank/CodeChef handler.
