INTRODUCTION
“Don’t count the problem,do the solution”.
Problem Statement:
  • Participants will be organised into groups.
  • One picture will be shown to them and in the picture there will be different conditions/problems that will be shown in that picture.
  • According to the conditions provide they will discuss the picture among themselves and then using their own intellect,they have to make strategy,planning and will show how they will manage in tackling the problem to give the maximum output i.e, the problem is solved with maximum accuracy.
  • This type of event helps in teaching students how they can coordinate with each other when they will need to,also it helps in making quick and effective decisions.
